# Quick Start - Portfolio Builder

Welcome to your portfolio platform! Here's the 60-second setup:

## Step 1: View Templates
When you open the app, you'll see the landing page with "Create Your Portfolio For Free" and a "Start Building" button. Click it to see all 6 portfolio templates.

## Step 2: Choose a Template
Browse the template previews. Each shows:
- Design style
- Example content
- "Use This Template" button

The **Neo-Brutalist Minimalist** is our featured template - it's modern, clean, and professional.

## Step 3: Fill Your Information

### Basic Info Tab
- Upload a profile photo
- Enter your name and title
- Write your bio
- Add email, phone, and location

### Services Tab
- Add 3-5 services you offer
- Each service has a name, description, and emoji icon

### Projects Tab
- Add 2-3 of your best projects
- Include project name, description, image, and link

### Social Media Tab
- Link to your social profiles
- Add Twitter, LinkedIn, GitHub, Instagram, Facebook

## Step 4: Create Your URL

At the top of the form, enter your portfolio URL:
- **Example**: `john-designer` ‚Üí `yoursite.com/portfolio/john-designer`
- Click "Check" to verify it's available
- See "URL is available!" confirmation

## Step 5: Launch Your Portfolio

Click "Launch My Portfolio" when done. You'll automatically be redirected to your new portfolio page!

## Step 6: Share

Your portfolio is now live at: **`yoursite.com/portfolio/your-slug`**

Share this link with:
- Potential clients
- Recruiters
- Social media followers
- Email signature
- Your resume

## Common Questions

**Q: Can I change my portfolio after publishing?**
A: Yes! Just fill the form again and click "Launch My Portfolio" - it will update your existing portfolio.

**Q: Can I change my URL slug?**
A: Currently, you'd need to create a new portfolio with a different slug. We're adding URL changes soon!

**Q: Where can I see my portfolio?**
A: Open `yoursite.com/portfolio/your-slug` in any browser.

**Q: Can I use my own domain?**
A: Custom domains are coming soon!

**Q: How do I delete my portfolio?**
A: Contact support and we can remove it.
