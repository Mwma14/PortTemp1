# Portfolio Platform - Complete Setup Guide

Your portfolio builder platform is now ready! Here's everything you need to know to start using it.

## üöÄ Quick Start

1. **Access the Platform**: Open your Replit app in the browser
2. **Browse Templates**: View the 6 available portfolio templates
3. **Select a Template**: Click "Use This Template" on any design
4. **Fill in Your Details**: Complete the portfolio builder form with your information
5. **Create Your URL**: Give your portfolio a unique web address (slug)
6. **Launch**: Click "Launch My Portfolio" to publish
7. **Share**: Your portfolio is now live at `yoursite.com/portfolio/your-slug`

## üìã Portfolio Builder Form

### Basic Information Tab
- **Profile Image**: Upload a professional photo (shows as avatar and profile picture)
- **Portfolio Title**: Your name or business name
- **Subtitle**: Your role, tagline, or professional title
- **About**: Your bio and professional background
- **Email**: Contact email address
- **Phone**: Contact phone number
- **Location**: Your city and country

### Services Tab
- Add up to 8 services you offer
- Each service has a name, description, and emoji icon
- Click "Add Service" to add more
- Use the trash icon to remove services

### Projects Tab
- Showcase your portfolio work
- Each project includes:
  - **Project Name**: Title of your work
  - **Description**: What the project was about
  - **Project Image**: Screenshot or preview image
  - **Project Link**: URL to live project or case study
  - **Tags**: Skills or categories (e.g., "Design", "Frontend")

### Social Media Tab
- Add links to your social profiles
- Supported platforms: Twitter, LinkedIn, GitHub, Instagram, Facebook
- Each platform shows as a clickable button on your portfolio

## üåê Portfolio URL (Slug)

Your portfolio needs a unique web address:
- **Format**: `yoursite.com/portfolio/your-slug`
- **Requirements**:
  - Minimum 3 characters
  - Only letters, numbers, and hyphens allowed
  - Convert to lowercase automatically
  - No spaces

**Example slugs:**
- `john-smith` ‚Üí `yoursite.com/portfolio/john-smith`
- `creative-agency` ‚Üí `yoursite.com/portfolio/creative-agency`
- `portfolio2024` ‚Üí `yoursite.com/portfolio/portfolio2024`

**To Check Availability:**
1. Enter your desired slug
2. Click the "Check" button
3. Get instant feedback if it's available

## üêõ Troubleshooting

### Portfolio Won't Save
- Ensure all required fields are filled (title, email, etc.)
- Check that your URL slug is available (click "Check")
- Verify your internet connection

### Images Not Displaying
- Try uploading a different image
- Ensure image file size is under 2MB
- Try clearing your browser cache

### URL Slug Already Taken
- Choose a different slug (e.g., add your year or location)
- The platform prevents duplicate URLs automatically

### Services/Projects Not Showing
- Make sure you clicked "Add" after entering details
- Click the "Save Portfolio" button to finalize
